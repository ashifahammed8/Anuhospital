Department Of Joint Replacement & Trauma


Dr.M.Vinod Kumar


M.S.(GEN SURG), M.S.(ORTHO) M.CH.(ORTHO)
Consultant Ortho, Spine & joint Replacement Surgeon

ANU Hospitals offer comprehensive orthopedics care and non-invasive diagnostic services for our patients. This includes consultation with one of our orthopedician as well as an ongoing evaluation and management of bones/ spine/ musculature / knee / joint problems. We believe that prevention of any disease offers the best outcome and we at ANU Hospitals provide the consultation with the orthopediation & State-of-the-art diagnostic services, X-rays, BMD (Bone Mineral Density) test, Physiotherapy for the preventive care.



Facilities Available:-
  • Total Knee Replacement
  • Total Hip Replacement
  • Trauma
  • Spine Surgeries
